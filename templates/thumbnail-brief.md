a few days compare CTR in YouTube Studio. Keep what wins, repeat the pattern.

## Rules of thumb
1. It must be readable as a tiny image on a phone.
2. Don't put words the title already says — add NEW information.
3. Faces with strong expressions usually beat static objects.
4. Your thumbnails should look like a family — a consistent style builds a brand.
