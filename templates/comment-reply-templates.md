# Comment Reply Templates

Replying to comments fast (especially in the first hour) tells YouTube your
video sparks conversation, and it makes real viewers come back. These are
starting points — always make them sound like a human, not a robot.

## To a positive comment
- "Really glad this helped! What part are you going to try first?"
- "Thank you 🙏 — what would you want me to cover next?"

## To a question
- "Great question! Short answer: [answer]. I might do a full video on this."
- "[Answer]. Did that clear it up, or do you want me to go deeper?"

## To criticism (stay calm — never argue)
- "Fair point, thanks for the honest feedback. I'll keep that in mind next time."
- "Appreciate you saying so — what would you have wanted instead?"

## To grow the conversation (pin one of these)
- "What's your biggest struggle with [topic]? I read every reply."
- "If you could only fix ONE thing about [topic], what would it be?"

## What NOT to do
- Don't beg for subscribers in every reply — it reads as desperate.
- Don't paste the exact same reply everywhere — YouTube can flag it as spam.
- Don't delete honest criticism; respond gracefully instead.
